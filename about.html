Your Weather Companion

Our website features a Real-Time Weather Detector that monitors local conditions to keep you informed and ready. Based on those live data readings, the website comes with a Tips and Suggestions tab that offers daily recommendations and protocols that suggests the best ways to handle the current weather, like what to wear, as well as providing quick tips on how to stay safe and eco-friendly during extreme weather changes. Moreover, we have added a Dynamic News Feed that keeps you in the loop with everything happening in the world of climate change.

Our Mission

Bridging the gap in climate intelligence.

Thrive was born from the need to address SDG 13: Climate Action. Many urban and coastal communities face increasing weather risks without the tools to act quickly.

Turn one's awareness into action

We want this to be a go-to spot for anyone who wants to stay updated and help the planet but doesn't know where to start. By providing informational news and survival tips, we’re helping the community to build a resilient foundation to climate changes. Our mission is to show that staying informed about global warming can be easier, it can be as simple as checking the latest news and taking into account the suggestions on the tips section.
